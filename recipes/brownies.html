Brownies

Description

These cake-like brownies are the most delicious, fudgy treats that I have ever eaten. They are so moist and super quick and easy to make. For best results, use Dutch-processed cocoa. For less cake-like brownies, use all-purpose flour instead of self-rising.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup self-rising flour
  • ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts (Optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1 : Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8x8- or 9x9-inch baking pan.
  2. Step 2 : Beat together sugar and butter in a medium bowl. Add eggs and mix well. Combine flour, cocoa, and salt; stir into sugar mixture. Mix in vanilla, then stir in walnuts. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
  3. Step 3 : Bake in the preheated oven until edges are firm, 25 to 30 minutes. Cool before cutting into squares.
